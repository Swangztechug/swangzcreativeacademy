Brand & Identity Design

Format

Studio sessions

Weekly live critique + guided work blocks you can repeat.

Outcome

A full identity system

Level

Beginner → intermediate

Syllabus

What you’ll build

You’ll go from strategy to a polished identity system with applications that look ready for a real client.

Week 1 · Brand strategy & references ▾
  • Define audience, tone, constraints, and visual territory.
  • Build a reference library and a direction board.
Week 2 · Logo exploration ▾
  • Sketching, mark vs wordmark, and iteration strategy.
  • Create a logo suite (primary, secondary, icon).
Week 3 · Typography & hierarchy ▾
  • Type pairing, scale, and layout rhythm.
  • Build a simple type system you can reuse.
Week 4 · Color & components ▾
  • Color system, accessibility basics, and components.
  • Patterns, textures, and supporting graphics.
Week 5 · Applications ▾
  • Design 3–5 realistic touchpoints (social, packaging, web, print).
  • Create mockups that tell a cohesive story.
Week 6 · Case study & presentation ▾
  • Write your narrative and curate the work.
  • Final presentation + critique and polish.
