Syllabus

What you’ll build

A progression from motion fundamentals to refined sequences ready for your portfolio.

Weeks 1–2 · Foundations ▾
  • Timing, spacing, ease-in/out, anticipation.
  • Micro-interactions and motion “do/don’t” studies.
Weeks 3–5 · Systems ▾
  • Motion language: rules for transitions and components.
  • UI motion studies and iterative critique.
Weeks 6–8 · Reel piece ▾
  • One hero animation with polish pass.
  • Exporting, presentation, and final critique.
