Portfolio · 8 min read

Portfolios that feel like you

Curate fewer projects with stronger narrative—so your work reads as intentional, not random.

A strong portfolio isn’t a dump of everything you’ve ever made. It’s a story: what you care about, how you think, and what outcomes you can ship.

Pick 3–5 projects max

Show range with intention: one brand system, one layout-heavy piece, one motion or photo story, and one “wild card.”

Use a consistent case study format

  • Problem / brief
  • Your role + constraints
  • Process highlights (only what matters)
  • Final work + what you learned

Checklist

If your portfolio needs a refresh, start with: fewer projects, clearer writing, and better mockups.
