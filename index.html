Career Paths in Music

Discover the diverse career opportunities available to graduates of our programs.

Music Producer

Create and produce music for artists, films, and commercial projects. Work in studios or independently.

Average Salary: $50k - $150k+

Sound Engineer

Mix and master audio recordings, work in live sound, or specialize in post-production for media.

Average Salary: $40k - $120k+

Performing Artist

Pursue a career as a solo artist, band member, or session musician. Perform live and record albums.

Average Salary: $30k - $200k+

Music Teacher

Educate the next generation of musicians in schools, private studios, or online platforms.

Average Salary: $35k - $80k+

Music Composer

Write original music for films, TV shows, video games, and commercial advertising.

Average Salary: $45k - $150k+

Music Director

Lead musical productions, conduct orchestras, or direct music programs for organizations.

Average Salary: $50k - $100k+
